WHY THIS STRUCTURE

Better for users, stronger for indexing

The homepage still gives a fast overview, but these pages hold the detail Google needs to understand the studio's real commercial scope. Instead of hiding the strongest intent inside buttons and modal panels, the site now exposes stable URLs that can be linked, crawled, indexed, and revisited.

That matters especially on GitHub Pages, where the technical stack is already lightweight and crawl-friendly. The biggest SEO wins come from stronger page architecture, clearer internal links, and a sitemap that matches the content you genuinely want indexed.
