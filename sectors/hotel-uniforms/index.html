HOTEL UNIFORM SYSTEMS

Custom hotel uniforms built across departments, shifts, and guest touchpoints.

Hotel wardrobes need to hold together visually while working for very different roles, from front office to housekeeping and restaurant service. The objective is not just beautiful garments, but one coherent system that still performs department by department.

OPERATIONAL FIT

One identity, multiple operational realities

Hotels usually combine guest-facing elegance with high-frequency daily wear. Reception, concierge, bar, restaurant, housekeeping, and management all need garments that feel related without forcing the same solution onto every role.

The studio approach starts by mapping the rhythm of the property, the architecture, and the visible standard the hotel wants to hold. That lets the wardrobe stay aligned at brand level while remaining practical for each department.

Built for

Multi-department hospitality teams

Projects where front office, food and beverage, guest services, and support roles need a shared visual direction and a stable reorder system.

Operational focus

Operational focus

Hotel uniforms have to absorb long shifts, mixed role requirements, laundry pressure, and frequent guest interaction. That is why the garment mix, fit direction, and finishing decisions need to be made as a system rather than as isolated pieces.

Continuity

Continuity

Once the first run is approved, patterns, fabrics, trims, and role references are archived so future reorders can stay consistent even as the team expands or seasonal staffing changes.
